# Surrogate — Game Pitch (High level)

## Theme and technology
Surrogate is a third person action shooter built in **Unreal Engine 5** (this repo currently targets **UE 5.7**) and intended for PC and consoles.

The game follows a detective living in a post‑nuked world, investigating a lead on hundreds of missing people in a “Dead City” — a city that wasn’t destroyed, but is still abandoned.

## Gameplay
A mix of immersive silent exploration, resource gathering, and combat (ranged with a variety of guns + melee).

Inspirations include Max Payne, Dark Souls, the Batman Arkham series, and F.E.A.R. Combat is intense, challenging, and deliberately over‑the‑top.

Player abilities (target):
- Slow motion
- Shield block
- Evade / roll
- Grapple (pull enemies / reach traversal points)
- Powerful melee finisher

Weapons (target): pistols, shotguns, assault rifles, sniper rifles, each with unique characteristics and upgrades.

## Progression
Mostly linear progression with a few open “sandbox” areas.

Pacing is slow and methodical, with environment exploration and combat as the focus.

Environments include abandoned cities, underground tunnels, and desolate wastelands.

Enemies include soldiers, ninjas, robots, mechs, zombies, creatures, and more; the game features boss encounters that require full use of abilities.
